# Privacy Policy — MonkeyLex

**Last updated:** 1 May 2026
**App:** MonkeyLex (Android)
**Developer:** Kakoo Games
**Contact:** kakoogames@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information the MonkeyLex Android app
("the app", "we", "us") collects, why it is collected, how it is used,
and the choices you have. By installing or using MonkeyLex you agree to
the practices described below.

---

## 1. Information we collect

MonkeyLex is a single-player word-puzzle game. The app itself does not
require an account, does not ask you for any personal information, and
does not contain any user-to-user features.

We use the following third-party services that may collect information
automatically:

| Service | Provider | Data collected | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Google LLC | Advertising ID, IP address, coarse device/OS info, ad-interaction events | Serve and measure ads (rewarded ads for coins and hints) |
| Firebase Analytics | Google LLC | App-usage events (screens viewed, levels completed), pseudonymous app-instance ID, coarse device/OS info | Understand aggregate usage so we can improve the app |
| Firebase Crashlytics | Google LLC | Crash stack traces, device model, OS version, app version, breadcrumb logs leading to the crash | Diagnose and fix crashes |

We do **not** collect or store:
- Your name, email, phone number, address, or any other contact details
- Photos, contacts, calendars, microphone audio, or precise location
- Any data you type into the keyboard outside the app

All gameplay data (level progress, coins, hints, achievements, monkey
stage) is stored **locally on your device** in private app storage and
is removed when you uninstall the app.

---

## 2. How we use the information

- **AdMob** uses the data to serve ads — personalized where you have
  consented, non-personalized otherwise.
- **Firebase Analytics** is used in aggregate form only, to understand
  which chapters are popular and where users drop off.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** uses crash data to help us fix bugs.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties
beyond the providers listed above.

---

## 3. Legal bases (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and
Switzerland:

- **Personalized ads:** processed only with your consent, gathered
  on first launch via the Google UMP consent form. You can change
  your choice at any time by reinstalling the app or by clearing app
  data, which will re-trigger the consent form on next launch.
- **Non-personalized ads, crash reporting, anonymous analytics:**
  processed under our legitimate interest in operating, supporting,
  and improving a free game.

---

## 4. Your choices

- **Reset your advertising ID** at any time:
  Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID.
- **Opt out of personalized ads:**
  Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" (or in
  the consent form on EU/EEA devices).
- **Stop crash and analytics reporting:** uninstall the app.

---

## 5. Children

MonkeyLex is rated for everyone and does not knowingly collect personal
information from children. We do not direct ads to children. If you
believe a child has provided information to us, contact us and we will
delete it.

---

## 6. Data retention

- **On-device data** (progress, coins, hints) is retained until you
  uninstall the app or clear app data.
- **AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Crashlytics data** is retained
  according to Google's standard retention policies; see
  [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  for details.

---

## 7. Security

All data transmitted to Google services is sent over HTTPS. Local data
is stored in Android's private app sandbox, accessible only to the
MonkeyLex app on your device.
