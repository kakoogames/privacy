# Privacy Policy for MonkeyLex: Word Puzzle Game

**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

**Developer:** KaKoo Games

This Privacy Policy describes how MonkeyLex ("the App") handles information when you use it.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
MonkeyLex does **not** require account creation, login, or any personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

### Automatically Collected Information
The App uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

**Google AdMob (Advertising)**
- Advertising ID
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address (for ad serving and geographic targeting)
- Ad interaction data

AdMob may use this information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent preferences. For more details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Firebase Analytics**
- Anonymous usage data (screens visited, features used)
- Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
- App performance metrics
- Session duration

This data is aggregated and anonymous — it cannot identify you personally. For more details, see [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

**Firebase Crashlytics**
- Crash logs and stack traces
- Device state at time of crash (OS version, device model, memory usage)
- Crashlytics installation UUID (anonymous, device-specific)

Crash data helps us fix bugs and improve app stability. For more details, see [Crashlytics Data Collection](https://firebase.google.com/docs/crashlytics/manage-crash-insights).

## How We Use Information

- To serve advertisements (AdMob)
- To understand how the App is used and improve it (Firebase Analytics)
- To identify and fix crashes and bugs (Firebase Crashlytics)

We do **not** sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties beyond the services listed above.

## Data Storage

- Game progress (level, coins, hints, achievements) is stored **locally on your device** using Android DataStore
- No game data is transmitted to any server
- If you uninstall the App, all local data is deleted

## Children's Privacy

MonkeyLex is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App serves ads through Google AdMob, which complies with applicable children's privacy regulations.

## Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data directly, there is typically no personal data to access, correct, or delete. For data collected by Google services, you can manage your data through your [Google Account settings](https://myaccount.google.com/).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
