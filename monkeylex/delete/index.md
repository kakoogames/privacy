# Data Deletion — MonkeyLex

**App:** MonkeyLex (Android)  
**Developer:** Kakoo Games  
**Contact:** kakoogames@gmail.com

---

## How to delete your data

### Local game data (progress, coins, achievements)

All gameplay data is stored **locally on your device**. To delete it:

1. **Uninstall the app** — this removes all local data automatically, OR
2. Go to **Settings → Apps → MonkeyLex → Storage → Clear Data**

No account or server-side game data exists.

---

### Third-party service data

MonkeyLex uses the following services that may store data on their servers:

| Service | How to delete |
|---------|--------------|
| **Google AdMob** | Reset your Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID. You can also opt out of ad personalization. |
| **Firebase Analytics** | Analytics data is aggregated and pseudonymous. It cannot be linked back to you individually. It is automatically deleted after 14 months. |
| **Firebase Crashlytics** | Crash data is anonymous and expires automatically. |
| **Google Play Games** | Revoke access: Settings → Google → Connected apps → MonkeyLex → Disconnect. Or visit [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). |
