# Data Deletion Request — GuessMe

**App:** GuessMe (Android)
**Developer:** Kakoo Games
**Contact:** kakoogames@gmail.com

This page explains how to delete data associated with the GuessMe app.

---

## 1. On-device data (instant, user-controlled)

All gameplay data (scores, streaks, statistics, settings, local logs) is
stored **only on your device** in GuessMe's private app storage.

**To delete it:**

- **Option A — Clear app data:**
  Android Settings → Apps → GuessMe → Storage → **Clear data**.
  Deletes everything instantly. The app reverts to a first-launch state.

- **Option B — Uninstall:**
  Long-press the GuessMe icon → Uninstall. This removes the app and
  every byte of its local data.

No further action is required for on-device data — it is never sent to
any server.

---

## 2. Google Play Games leaderboard score (if you signed in)

If you chose to sign in with Google Play Games Services to submit your
score to the public leaderboard:

- **To delete your submitted scores:** open the **Google Play Games**
  app → Profile → Settings → **Delete Play Games account & data**.
  This removes all scores across all Play Games titles, including
  GuessMe, from Google's servers.
- Alternatively, contact us at the email below and we will request
  removal of your GuessMe-specific score on your behalf.

---

## 3. Analytics and crash report data (Google Firebase)

If you did not opt out (see privacy policy for regional consent
details), GuessMe sends anonymous usage events and crash reports to
Firebase. These are pseudonymous — not tied to your name, email, or
account.

**To request deletion:**

Email us at **kakoogames@gmail.com** with the subject line
**"GuessMe data deletion request"**. Please include:

- The approximate date range you used the app (e.g. "April 2026")
- Your device model if you know it (helps narrow the Crashlytics records)

We will file a deletion request with Firebase within **30 days** and
confirm completion by email. You do **not** need to provide your name,
email address, or any personal information beyond what's needed to
locate the records.

Firebase Analytics data is automatically purged after **14 months**
regardless. Crashlytics data is purged after **90 days** regardless.

---

## 4. Advertising data (Google AdMob)

Ad-related data (advertising ID, ad interactions) is collected by
Google AdMob on Google's servers, not ours.

**To delete or reset it:**

- **Reset your Android Advertising ID:**
  Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → **Reset advertising ID**.
- **Opt out of personalized ads entirely:**
  Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → **Delete advertising ID**.
- **Withdraw GDPR consent (EU/EEA/UK users):**
  Clear app data (see section 1) and relaunch — the consent form will
  reappear on next launch and you can refuse.

---

## 5. Questions or escalation

If any of the above steps do not work for you, or you'd like us to
handle the deletion on your behalf, email:

**kakoogames@gmail.com**

Subject: `GuessMe data deletion request`

We respond within **5 business days** and complete deletion within
**30 days**.
